What is the minimum?

Three standard rooms, currently $165 inside the primary service area.

How long does drying take?

Typical drying time is about 2–4 hours, depending on airflow, humidity, carpet density and the amount of water required.

Which areas do you serve?

Edwardsville, Glen Carbon, Collinsville, Alton, O'Fallon and nearby Metro East communities.

Can every stain be removed?

No. Bleach marks, permanent dye damage and deep contamination in padding or subfloor may not be fully removable. Photos help us set expectations.
