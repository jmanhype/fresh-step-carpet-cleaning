Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long does a typical job take?

Most jobs take 1.5-3 hours depending on size. A 3-bedroom house is typically 3-4 hours including setup and drying time.

How long until my carpet is dry?

Our commercial-grade equipment extracts 95% of moisture. Most carpets are dry in 2-4 hours, not 24+ hours like rental machines.

Do I need to move furniture?

Light furniture (chairs, small tables) we can move for you. Heavy items (couches, beds) please move before we arrive or we'll clean around them.

Are your chemicals safe for kids and pets?

Yes. We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for children, pets, and allergy sufferers.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy, we'll come back and re-clean for free. If still not happy, you don't pay.

What's your minimum job?

$80 minimum (covers 2 rooms or 1 room + hallway). This ensures we can deliver professional results without cutting corners.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! 10% off for first-time customers, 15% off for 5+ rooms, and volume discounts for landlords/property managers.
